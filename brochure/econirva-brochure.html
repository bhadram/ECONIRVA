ABOUT US

ECONIRVA Bio Solutions Pvt. Ltd. is a Central Pollution Control Board (CPCB) Certified manufacturer of compostable plastics. We specialize in producing high-quality biodegradable and compostable bags that serve today's needs without harming tomorrow's world.

At ECONIRVA, we believe sustainability should be thoughtfully designed, responsibly manufactured, and accessible to all. Every bag we produce is a step toward reducing plastic pollution and protecting our environment. Our products are carefully developed to replace conventional plastic while maintaining strength, safety, and reliability.
